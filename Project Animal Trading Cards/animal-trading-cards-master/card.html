Capuchin Monkey

Capuchin monkeys pee on their hands to wash their feet.

  • Family: Cebidae
  • Average Height: 43 cm
  • Average Weight: 5.5 pounds
  • Habitat: Tropical forests

Agile and lean, capuchin monkeys weigh only 3 - 9 pounds (1.36 - 4.9 kilograms).
The fur of the capuchin monkey varies, but is most commonly seen with cream or light tan coloring
around the face, neck and shoulders.
The rest of its coat is dark brown.
